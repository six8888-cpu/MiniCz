Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

Introduction

Divination Pet ("the Extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our Chrome browser extension.

Summary: We do NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The extension operates entirely locally on your device.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information.

The Extension only uses Chrome's local storage API to store:

  • A single flag indicating whether you have clicked the pet for the first time (to play a welcome voice)

This data is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to any server.

Permissions Used

The Extension requires the following permission:

  • storage: Used to save the first-click status locally. This is purely for enhancing user experience and does not involve any personal data.

Data Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties because we do not collect any data.

Data Security

Since all data remains on your local device and no information is transmitted over the internet, your data is as secure as your device.

Children's Privacy

The Extension does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13 years of age.
